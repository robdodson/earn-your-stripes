Be Social

How do you tap into the developer community at large to learn about trends and find mentorship.

Write. Constantly.

This is probably the most important slide in here.

Start a blog. Try to write 15 blog posts in 1 month. Or try writing every day for a month. Don't be embarrased. Treat your blog like a journal.

Coderwall

Like Tumblr for developers Spending a bunch of time on your blog theme is like spending hours buying fancy gym shoes and never going to the gym.
